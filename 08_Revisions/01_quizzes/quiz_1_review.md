## Foundations Checklist

Before moving on, make sure you can explain these like a 5th grader:
- **ML:** A computer learning from experience without being told exactly what to do.
- **Supervised Learning:** Learning with a teacher (labeled data).
- **Unsupervised Learning:** Learning by finding patterns on your own.
- **Loss Function:** The "Scoreboard" that tells the computer how many mistakes it made.

## High-Level Summary

Machine Learning is just a giant cycle:
1. **Gather Data** (Linear Algebra)
2. **Guess a Model** (Functions & Geometry)
3. **Measure the Error** (Loss Functions)
4. **Fix the Model** (Calculus & Optimization)
5. **Repeat!**

## Final Review Question

**Question:** If you want to find the relationship between "Price" and "Size," but your data is a messy cloud of points, which mathematical tool should you use to find the **best-fit line**?

**Solution:**
You should use **Least Squares Optimization** (Category 03). It will find the line that makes the "Total Square Error" as small as possible.
